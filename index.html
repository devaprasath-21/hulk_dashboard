ORIGIN STORY

☢️

Gamma Radiation

Bruce Banner was exposed to an enormous dose of gamma radiation during a bomb test, transforming him forever.

Dr. Bruce Banner, a brilliant physicist working for the US Army, shielded teenager Rick Jones from a gamma bomb explosion. The gamma radiation rewrote Banner's DNA, triggering a Jekyll-and-Hyde transformation whenever his emotions spike — especially anger.

🧬

Bruce Banner

One of the greatest scientific minds on Earth — and the unwilling host of the most powerful Avenger.

Robert Bruce Banner holds multiple PhDs in nuclear physics, biochemistry, and engineering. His genius-level intellect rivals Tony Stark. In his Banner form he is able to operate the Avengers' most advanced technology and formulate cures for gamma-induced mutations.

🛡️

Founding Avenger

A core member of the original Avengers alongside Iron Man, Thor, Ant-Man, and Wasp.

Hulk was one of the original five Avengers assembled to battle Loki. Despite his troubled nature, Earth's Mightiest Heroes have repeatedly called on Hulk to stand against cosmic-level threats — from Thanos to world-ending Celestials.
